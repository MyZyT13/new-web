My Ridez

For PC:

  • Click the Car/Bike
  • Drag the Car/Bike using your mouse.
  • Use the mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
  • Right-click to flip the car.

For Mobile:

  • Press the Car/Bike
  • Drag the Car/Bike using your finger.
  • Pinch to zoom in and out.
  • Double-tap to flip the car.
